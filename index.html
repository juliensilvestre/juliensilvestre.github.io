About Me

Hey there! I’m Francisco and I’m a Technical Artist and Unreal Engine Specialist with a passion for creative technologies.

I’ve spent the last few years diving deep into UE and working on AAA game titles. I love experimenting with proceduralism, generative art, shader art, and exploring new mediums inside and outside of games.

Alongside creating, I’ve had the opportunity to teach, give talks, participate in Game Jams and share my knowledge with other passionate artists and developers. Whether I’m collaborating with a team or working solo, I thrive on bringing innovative ideas to life and building experiences that encourage people to explore, create, and immerse themselves.

Currently freelancing, I’m always excited to chat about new opportunities. Feel free to reach out!
